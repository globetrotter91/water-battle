WELCOME TO WATER BATTLE!

Enter your name and click start.

Rules are pretty simple.

Use arrow keys to move your ship in the ocean.

Use space bar to shoot at an angle of 30 deg.

Use shift key to shoot at an angle of 45 deg.

Use enter key to shoot at an angle of 60 deg.

Shoot the ships when you see them.

You get 10 lives when you start. Getting shot by someone reduces your lives.

Successfully shooting someone will increase your score.

So good luck shooting!
